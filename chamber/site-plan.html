Ibadan Chamber of Commerce – Site Plan

Site Name

Ibadan Chamber of Commerce

This name represents the main organization being supported by the website. It clearly identifies the chamber and the city it serves, making it easy for local businesses and visitors to recognize.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote business development and economic growth in Ibadan. The site will provide business listings, membership information, local events, community resources, and opportunities for entrepreneurs.

Scenarios

  • “How can I register my business to become a member of the Ibadan Chamber of Commerce?”
  • “Where can I find the directory of local businesses in Ibadan?”
  • “What upcoming events or business seminars are happening in Ibadan?”

Color Scheme

Chosen colors for the website:

Dark Green (#1b5e20) – used for headings, navigation, and accents.

Light Yellow (#fff59d) – used for background sections and subtle highlights.

These colors reflect growth, community development, and a welcoming professional environment suitable for a chamber of commerce website.

Typography

Poppins – used for headings and navigation items.

Roboto – used for body text to ensure readability across all devices.
