Ibadan Chamber of Commerce – Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Ibadan Chamber of Commerce

Optional Domain: ibadanchamber.org

This name represents the official online hub of Ibadan’s business community. The goal is to promote local economic development, connect entrepreneurs, and encourage collaboration between businesses and residents.

Site Purpose

The Ibadan Chamber of Commerce website will serve as a business resource center for entrepreneurs and residents. It will include local events, business directories, and information about joining the chamber. The site aims to promote local trade, foster business growth, and strengthen community engagement.

Target Market

Business owners, entrepreneurs, investors, and community members within Ibadan and surrounding regions who are interested in local business development and networking opportunities.

Site Goals

  • Provide business resources and networking opportunities for members.
  • Encourage new businesses to establish in Ibadan.
  • Increase visibility of local businesses and the chamber’s influence.
  • Promote community events and local shopping.

User Personas

  • Small Business Owner (Aisha): A 33-year-old fashion designer looking for ways to promote her boutique and connect with other small business owners.
  • Corporate Executive (Kunle): A 45-year-old manager seeking partnership opportunities and local investment prospects.
  • New Resident (David): A newcomer to Ibadan wanting to discover trusted local services, events, and business listings.

Scenarios

  • A local entrepreneur visits the website to learn about chamber membership benefits, fees, and how to join.
  • A resident wants to find upcoming events and networking sessions hosted by the chamber.
  • An investor explores the site to understand the local business landscape and available opportunities in Ibadan.

SEO Plan

  • Use relevant keywords like “Ibadan business network,” “Ibadan Chamber of Commerce,” and “local business community.”
  • Verify the site through Google Business Profile and embed Google Analytics on all pages.
  • Encourage backlinks from member businesses to strengthen SEO rankings.
  • Publish news articles and event updates regularly to maintain active site content.

Design Brief

  • Primary Color: #0066CC (Professional blue for trust and stability)
  • Secondary Color: #F4B400 (Vibrant gold for highlights and accents)
  • Background Color: #FDFDFD (Light, clean background for readability)
  • Text Color: #333333 (Dark gray for easy reading)
  • Font Family: Poppins (for headings) and Open Sans (for body text)
